Oregon State University

A classic choice for most oregon residents going into the technical fields. OSU offers an electrical and computer engineering major, allowing me to pursue my love of engineering circuts and the intrigue of computer programming all at once. Nestled in Corvallis Oregon with a relatively affordable tuition and a rough ranking of 300 in engineering schools. OSU offers graduate programs in a wide variety of the technical fields, including computer science, electrical and computer engineering and robotics. OSU provides an excellent baseline for my future transfer prospects.
