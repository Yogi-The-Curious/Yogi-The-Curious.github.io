Plant Irrigation

A smart irrigation system that checks the moisture content of the plant soil twice a day, and irrigates plant to a pre set amount accordingly, arduino sleeps in between checks to conserve power. Unfortunately the project proved to be more suited to the scale of a backyard rather then a desktop as originally intended. Fully functional though. Check out the test run linked in the READ ME on github.
